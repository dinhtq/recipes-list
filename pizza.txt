sauce:

1 can (14-15 oz) of crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce (use good-quality canned tomatoes for better flavor)
1-2 cloves of garlic, minced
1-2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil (optional)
1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
Salt and pepper to taste
Red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)

Pizza Dough Ingredients:
2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
1 ½ cups warm water (110°F or 45°C)
3 ½ to 4 cups all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon sugar
2 teaspoons salt

Crust:
Knead the dough for 9 minutes.
Stretch it into a flat circle.
Add some seasoning if you like.
